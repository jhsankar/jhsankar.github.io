About Me

Hello!

I'm Jhanavi Sankar, a public policy and data professional with a passion for turning complex data into actionable insights that drive equitable outcomes. I'm a CMU Heinz graduate student, focused on how data and emerging technologies can improve public outcomes—especially in education, workforce development, and responsible AI policy.

My work is grounded in applied problem-solving: building analytics workflows, evaluating programs, and creating dashboards and decision tools that stakeholders can actually use. I've worked on projects spanning student support systems (MTSS), education program evaluation, and workforce analytics, often bridging quantitative analysis with real-world constraints such as imperfect data, implementation gaps, and policy trade-offs.

In this portfolio, you'll see projects that show my approach. I begin with a clear question, use a straightforward method, and share results in plain language so the analysis can lead to real action.

I also hold a B.A. (Honors) in Sociology and History from Lady Shri Ram College, Delhi University, grounding my quantitative work in a deep understanding of social systems and equity.
